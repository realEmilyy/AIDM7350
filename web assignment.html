1988 animated film directed by Tsuyoshi Nisaki

It is an animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki and voiced by Noriko Hidaka, Chika Sakamoto, and Shigori Itsui. It was released in Japan on April 16, 1988. The HD remastered version was released in China on December 14, 2018.

The film tells the story of Tatsuro Sobi's wife, Yasuko Sobi, who is ill in hospital, and his return to the countryside with her and her four-year-old sister, Mei Sobi.

Introduction of the author
Hayao Miyazaki

Born on January 5, 1941 in Bunkyo-ku, Tokyo, Japan, he is a Japanese animator, animation producer, cartoonist, animation director and animation screenwriter. He graduated from the Department of Political Economy, Gakushuin University, Tokyo, Japan.

In 2013, he directed "The Wind Rises", which won eight awards including the Best Animation Award at the 37th Japan Film Academy Awards and was also his last full-length work. He retired on September 6 of the same year.

On November 8, 2014, he received the Lifetime Achievement Award at the 87th Academy Awards.

Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation studio. Founded in mid-1985, it was originally affiliated with Tokuma Bookstore, located in Koganai City, near Tokyo, Japan, and has about 300 employees. It is coordinated by director Hayao Miyazaki and his colleagues Isao Takahata and Toshio Suzuki, etc., and composer Reya Hisaishi has produced film music for many Studio Ghibli works.

The studio logo is its representative work "My Neighbor Totoro", etc.
